About ESpec

Have you always wanted to buy Euro-Spec? The trim, the options, the tuning, the provenance that never made it stateside? That’s where we come in. ESpec is your experienced partner on the ground in Europe: we hunt the right examples, verify condition, negotiate the purchase, and coordinate everything from pickup to port. You stay in control with clear reports, photos, and transparent landed-cost estimates.

  • Trusted sourcing across dealers, private sellers, and auctions in EU/UK
  • Preliminary on-site inspections (photos, video, paint readings) + certified checks via TÜV/DEKRA partners
  • Purchase support, documents, deregistration, and export customs
  • Inland transport to port and handoff to your preferred shipper
